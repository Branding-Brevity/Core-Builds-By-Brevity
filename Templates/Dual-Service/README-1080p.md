# ⛓️ Dual Core 1080p SDR (Hybrid)

**Maximum link availability for standard hardware, fortified with RD infringement protections.**

## 🎯 Ideal Use Case
Designed for standard 1080p displays and Android projectors, but built for power users who maintain active subscriptions to both TorBox and Real-Debrid. It ensures you never hit a "No Streams Found" error by merging the massive caches of both services.

## ⚙️ What it Does
* **Hardware Safe:** Locks resolution to 1080p, forces SDR, and strips out heavy codecs (AV1/TrueHD/DTS-HD) that cause stuttering.
* **Dual-Cache Merging:** Pulls and deduplicates links from both TorBox and Real-Debrid simultaneously.
* **RD Safety Scrub:** Automatically filters out targeted `WEB-DL`, `WEBRip`, and streaming platform tags on Real-Debrid links to prevent the May 2026 "Infringing File" playback errors.
* **Bandwidth Friendly:** Capped at 60 Mbps for smooth wireless streaming.
