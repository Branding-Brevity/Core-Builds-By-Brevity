# ⛓️ Dual Core 4K Unleashed (Hybrid)

**The ultimate 4K powerhouse, maximizing premium links while shielding against broken streams.**

## 🎯 Ideal Use Case
The definitive build for home theater enthusiasts who refuse to compromise on quality and want the absolute deepest pool of 4K Remuxes available. Ideal for premium 4K TVs and dedicated surround sound setups, utilizing both TorBox and Real-Debrid.

## ⚙️ What it Does
* **Unleashed Fidelity:** Prioritizes 4K Remuxes, Dolby Vision, HDR10+, and lossless audio formats (Atmos, TrueHD, DTS:X).
* **Dual-Cache Merging:** Aggregates TorBox's Usenet dominance with Real-Debrid's massive torrent cache.
* **RD Safety Scrub:** Implements custom logic to silently hide Real-Debrid files affected by the May 2026 infringement takedowns, leaving only the playable BluRay links.
* **Maximum Bandwidth:** 150 Mbps ceiling to handle uncompressed media seamlessly.
