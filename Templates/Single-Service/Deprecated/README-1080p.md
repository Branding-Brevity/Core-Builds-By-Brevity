# 📦 Core Nexus 1080p SDR (TorBox Exclusive)

**The frictionless daily driver for standard displays and limited hardware.**

## 🎯 Ideal Use Case
This template is built specifically for standard Google TV dongles, older televisions, hotel Wi-Fi, and Android projectors. It is designed to run perfectly on hardware that chokes on heavy files or lacks the licensing to decode high-end audio and video formats. 

## ⚙️ What it Does
* **Privacy First:** Exclusively uses TorBox for maximum privacy and zero logging.
* **Strict Resolution Cap:** Hard-locks maximum resolution to 1080p.
* **SDR Forced:** Strips out all Dolby Vision (DV) and HDR files to prevent washed-out colors on standard displays.
* **Codec & Audio Safe:** Filters out AV1 encodes, TrueHD, and DTS-HD to prevent audio stuttering and hardware decoding crashes on low-power devices.
* **Bandwidth Friendly:** Imposes a strict 60 Mbps bitrate cap to ensure smooth playback on standard Wi-Fi networks.
