# 📦 Core Nexus 4K Home Theater (TorBox Exclusive)

**The unleashed master build for high-end audio and visual setups.**

## 🎯 Ideal Use Case
This template is engineered for premium home theater environments. If you are running an Nvidia Shield TV Pro, a high-end Samsung/LG OLED, or a dedicated surround sound system (like a tuned JBL soundbar or discrete receiver), this is your build. 

## ⚙️ What it Does
* **Privacy First:** Exclusively uses TorBox for maximum privacy and zero logging.
* **Unleashed Fidelity:** Actively hunts and prioritizes 4K Remuxes.
* **Premium Formats:** Pushes Dolby Vision (Profile 7/8), HDR10+, Dolby Atmos, and DTS:X tracks to the very top of your results.
* **Maximum Bandwidth:** Raises the bitrate ceiling to 150 Mbps to accommodate massive, uncompressed Blu-ray rips.
