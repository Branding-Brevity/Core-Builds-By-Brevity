# 🎬 WuPlay Integration & Branding Guide

The **Core Builds** and **Core Formatters** are engineered to integrate seamlessly into the WuPlay media application. Rather than feeling like a bolted-on third-party addon, these configurations are designed to match WuPlay's native aesthetic, ensuring a premium, cohesive experience on your home theater or projector.

## 📐 The Design Language

To maintain brand consistency across the UI, our custom formatters adhere strictly to the established WuPlay visual identity.

### Typography Hierarchy
If you are tweaking the formatters or building your own custom layout for AIOStreams, we highly recommend adhering to this typographic structure so the stream list doesn't clash with the WuPlay interface:
* **Montserrat:** Reserved exclusively for primary brand logos and top-level structural headers. Its geometric weight grounds the UI.
* **Inter:** Strictly implemented for all subtext, file metadata, and secondary readouts. Its high legibility ensures rapid scanning of codecs and file sizes from a distance.

### Visual Badging
The **Zenith Diamond** and **Auburn Tiger** formatters utilize high-contrast, scalable badging. Because WuPlay handles massive libraries of high-fidelity media, the badging system strips out raw filename clutter and replaces it with clean, color-coded tags for:
* **Resolution:** (4K, 1080p)
* **Dynamic Range:** (DV, HDR10+)
* **Audio Codecs:** (Atmos, TrueHD, DTS:X)

## ⚙️ How to Link Core Builds to WuPlay

Integrating your AIOStreams configuration into the WuPlay app takes just a few seconds.

1. **Configure Your Build:** Open your AIOStreams dashboard, import your chosen Core Build (e.g., `Core Nexus 4K Home Theater`), and paste your custom Formatter JSON into the advanced settings.
2. **Generate the Manifest:** Once your API keys are entered and your settings are saved, click **Install** or copy the **Manifest URL** generated by AIOStreams.
3. **Add to WuPlay:** * Open the WuPlay application on your device.
   * Navigate to the Addons or Integrations menu.
   * Paste the AIOStreams Manifest URL into the search/import bar.
   * Click Install. 

Your WuPlay stream lists will instantly update to feature the optimized caching and custom typography of the Core Builds.
